Upgrade Spend Window — "The Refit"

Designed for the new economy (GDD E2): each XP level banks a pre-rolled offer of 3 boons from 3 distinct categories — qualitative, Hades-style build-definers, no heal, no percentages. Interaction is locked: HOLD CTRL opens · release dismisses · number key spends — never a toggle; emptying the bank also closes. Layout is locked: three cards side by side (vertical list rejected). Visual register is the hotbar's Afterimage language — floating 1px outlines, no shared panel, the battle stays visible between and around the cards.
