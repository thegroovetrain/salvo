Three sequential frames of the same death (Journey A's Marco register: Cyan Gunboat, torpedoed by a stalking Torpedo Boat), authored at 1920×1080 and shown at the same 0.75× scale. The design intent is "losing into learning": ~5 s ritardando with guns live → the fog drops off the whole ocean and the camera zooms out (the omniscient reveal IS the death screen) → a results modal that costs one key (Enter) to re-queue. Frames follow the spine where the HUD composite is superseded: vertical HP Rail, Q/E/R/F key glyphs, "HOLD SPACE" copy, four slots, no supply ghost. Amber chips/tags = [PROPOSAL] composition choices; blue = PLACEHOLDER copy; grey = spine silences annotated for Eric.
